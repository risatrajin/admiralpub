Life at the Admiral

Our Story

Burnaby's Favourite
Local Pub

Tucked on East Hastings, the Admiral Pub & Grill has been a cornerstone of the Burnaby community for over two decades. Warm wooden walls, the crack of a hockey puck on TV, a cold pint of BC craft beer in hand — this is what we're about.

We believe a great pub is more than food and drink. It's a gathering place — for first dates, playoff games, birthday bashes, and Tuesday night trivia. Live cover bands on weekends, weekly specials, and staff who know your name.
